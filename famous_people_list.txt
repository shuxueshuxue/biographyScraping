TOTAL: 125 famous people across diverse backgrounds

SELECTION CRITERIA:
- Well-documented life stories with multiple biographical sources
- Significant adversity, failure, or challenges overcome
- Diverse range of challenge types (poverty, discrimination, health, failure, loss)
- Geographic and cultural diversity
- Mix of historical and contemporary figures
- Stories that can inspire people facing similar challenges today

SCRAPING PRIORITY (suggested order):
1. Start with top 20 most universally known (Jobs, Oprah, Einstein, etc.)
2. Add diversity across categories (not all business leaders)
3. Focus on individuals with rich online biographical content
4. Consider recency (more online sources for contemporary figures)
5. Balance inspirational with realistic struggle narratives
